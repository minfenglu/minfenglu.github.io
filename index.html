A little about me

I am enough of an artist to draw freely upon my imagination. The point is that when I see a sunset or a waterfall or something, for a split second it's so great, because for a little bit I'm out of my brain, and it's got nothing to do with me. I'm not trying to figure it out, you know what I mean? And I wonder if I can somehow find a way to maintain that mind stillness.

Elevating Lives: My Pursuit of Meaningful Social Change

Self-Driving Car

For half a decade, I was deeply immersed in the exciting and transformative world of autonomous vehicles, dedicating myself to the pursuit of revolutionizing transportation. Driven by an unwavering passion to enhance safety, efficiency, and sustainability, I've contributed to this cutting-edge technology that redefines the very concept of mobility. My journey with Zoox has not only affirmed my commitment to a greener, more accessible future, but it has also stoked my relentless drive to continue pushing the boundaries of innovation in transportation. The prospect of shaping a world where traffic congestion, carbon emissions, and road accidents are issues of the past fills me with profound enthusiasm and relentless motivation.

Online Education

In designing the PuStack app, I was fueled by a profound passion for democratizing education and reshaping the contours of online learning. My ambition was to cultivate an interactive, inclusive platform that fosters collaboration and engagement, thereby uniting individuals in their pursuit of knowledge. I fervently believe that education thrives not in isolation, but in shared experiences, and through PuStack, I have endeavored to embody this conviction. My aspiration is to show that education, when widely accessible and collaboratively pursued, can be a powerful equalizer and a catalyst for change. .

AI/ML Community

I find immense fulfillment in using artificial intelligence and machine learning to make everyday tasks simpler and more efficient. These technologies offer a unique opportunity to remove complexities, helping people accomplish more with less effort. My goal is to create tools that fit seamlessly into daily routines, making lives not just easier, but also more enjoyable. For me, it's about using these powerful tools to build a future where technology genuinely serves to enhance our human experience.
